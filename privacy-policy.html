Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 February 2026

Copas App ("the App", "we", "our") is designed to help users store and manage Copas locally on their devices. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  • Clipboard text is accessed only when you copy content.
  • All clipboard data is stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Clipboard Data

Clipboard data is used solely for the core functionality of the App:

  • Saving Copas
  • Displaying copied text back to the user

We do not share, upload, or sell clipboard data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device. The App does not use cloud storage, remote databases, or external servers.

Security relies on the built-in protection mechanisms of your operating system.

4. App Permissions

The App may request access to:

  • Clipboard access – required to save Copas.

These permissions are used strictly to provide app functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking or profiling services

6. User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You can clear Copas at any time within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
